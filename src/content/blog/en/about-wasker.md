# About Wasker the Vue

Wasker Vue is a modern portfolio and blog theme tailored for developers and designers. Built on **Vue 3** and powered by **Vite**, it aims to provide ultimate performance and a superior developer experience.

## Key Features

- **Vue 3 & Composition API**: Leverage the latest features of Vue 3 for cleaner code organization and easier logic reuse.
- **Responsive Design**: Provides a seamless browsing experience across mobile and desktop devices.
- **3D Interaction Graph**: Integrates a Force-Directed Graph to showcase your knowledge system or project connections in a unique way.
- **Content First**: Optimized typography and Markdown support make your articles and project showcases more attractive.
- **i18n Ready**: Built-in support for English and Chinese language switching to easily reach a global audience.

## Why Wasker Vue?

In this digital age, a unique and professional personal website is the best window to showcase yourself. Wasker Vue is more than just a template; it's a platform that can grow with you. It can be your tech blog, your design portfolio, or your playground for exploring Web technologies.
